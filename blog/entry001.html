New Things!

June 27, 2013


Posted by Andrew



Glyph Alpha 2 has just been released, and with it come a bunch of subtle changes and slight gameplay additions.
Here's the changelog:
  • Added energy fountains
    • hover over them to speed up your recharge rate by a factor of 2.
  • Changed recharge rates
    • 5, 15, 25, and 45 seconds from the previous bar respectively.
  • Gameplay progression
  • Added music
  • Added splash text
  • Most menus are navigable with the keyboard
  • New settings menu
  • New graphics options
  • Removed startup settings screen
  • Removed FPS meter
Known Bugs:
  • With a lot of enemies on the screen, framerates drop dramatically due to path calculations
    • The issue is not render time, but CPU calculation time. With 200+ enemies, the main thread can take between 3 and 5 seconds opposed to a preferred 15-25 ms.
  • Insane Mode is not a bug.
Exiciting.
So go ahead and download the new build from our wonderful downloads page which has recently seen a minor redesign. (Sorry to anyone who had a really small screen resolution)

You might also have noticed some comments above about "Insane Mode." If you have an older computer and don't want your house to burn down, I would stay very clear of that button. Here is what it looks like when the first enemies spawn:

If you do find yourself accidentally pressing that button, don't worry as the average length of a game in that mode is a little under 4 seconds. With god mode on. Also, pausing the game still spawns enemies. And they spawn every frame update. This means that in one second, about 60-100 enemies will spawn. You're welcome.
